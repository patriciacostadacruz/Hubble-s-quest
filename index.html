Jerry's rules

After years of fighting crime, this time you'll find Jerry in our lovely Barcelona. He only has one goal: ban robberies and allow Barcelona to find its tranquility once again. Will carteristas survive? This is up to you...
You cannot kill friendly NPCs nor get a point count below 0. If an enemy touches you, you automatically loose a point.

  • Press right arrow to shoot at enemies.
  • Press top arrow to jump over friendly NPCs.
  • Press enter to recharge.
